A Bit About Me..

I am currently a data scientist at SANDAG where I maintain and develop the models that forecast travel throughout the San Diego region. These models help San Diego better understand the impact of travel-related policy changes, infrastructure, or even emerging technologies such as autonomous vehicles.
My journey into data science began at the University of California San Diego, where I received my Bachelor's in Data Science. Whether it be deriving insights through data analysis, solving problems with machine learning, or telling a story through a visualization, data science has truly become a passion of mine.
